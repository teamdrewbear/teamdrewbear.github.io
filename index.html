OUR STORY

Teddy Bear shape

DREW was specifically designed to be a teddy bear, so that it can be huggable and familiar to the child, while not being overwhelming or overstimulating.

Pulsing

To help comfort the child and prevent meltdowns, DREW pulses at the rate of a healthy resting hearbeat.
